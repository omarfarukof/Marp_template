## Math

* ### Inline
Formula of derivatives: $\frac{d}{dx}(cos(x)) = -sin(x)$

* ### Full

$$
\frac{d}{dx}(tan(x)) = \frac{d}{dx}(\frac{sin(x)}{cos(x)})

= \frac{cos(x).\frac{d}{dx}(sin(x)) - \frac{d}{dx}(cos(x)).sin(x)}{cos^2(x)}
= \frac{cos^2(x) + sin^2(x)}{cos^2(x)}
$$

$$
= 1 + tan^2(x) = sec^2(x)
$$
